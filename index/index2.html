Unassuming diner serving American & Greek breakfast, lunch & dinner since 1969 in unfussy surrounds.

Greek plates for breakfast, lunch and dinner in its casual and relaxed setting. If you stop by Three Star Restaurant for

breakfast, you can enjoy such hearty and fresh offerings as the Spanish omelette, the chocolate chip pancakes with

Canadian bacon and the corned beef hash and two eggs.
